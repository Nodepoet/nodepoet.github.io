About Me

My interest in joining the tech industry started early in my youth. I was always fascinated by the power of advancing computer technologies, and their ability to create a profound impact on our daily lives. Whether it’s designing an application to facilitate productivity and financial growth, or building a silly game to lift our spirits after a hard day's work. Software development has always felt like an avenue through which I can express my creativity, and provide something of value to world.

Despite my love of technology, I also found myself driven by a strong desire to understand the nature of our world and the universe in which we live. I first graduated from West Chester University with a degree in Geoscience, accompanied by a minor in astronomy. I later returned to school to pursue a second degree in Computer Science from Oregon State University, which is where I discovered my true passion.

Following my education, I have applied those skills in a variety of personal and professional endeavors. While working as the VP of a moderately sized family-run construction and consulting company I created a modern website to accept housing applications, and field requests from potential clients. I then went on to attend an internship at TekCitadel design company, began offering my services as a freelance developer, and have continued to design applications and games for my own personal use.
